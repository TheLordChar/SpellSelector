Group 5 SpellSelector

All spells are shown on right of program.
To narrow down spells, click the buttons corresponding to tags you want.

At the bottom, select the number of levels in wizard you have.

Once done, click the SEND button.

The spells on the right will change to be those that correspond to at least one of your selected tags, sorted by least tags to most.

If you want to submit a different set of tags, click RESET to return the right screen to normal view.
